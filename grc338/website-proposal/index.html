Introduction

Delight

Delight is the first environmentally conscious and dairy free ice cream parlor in town. Using only the highest quality locally made ingredients in combination with various non-dariy milks including soy, coconut, and oat milk, our top-secret recipe creates sweet, creamy, and high-protein ice cream delights through the cleanest, most sustainable methods.

Target audience

People who use this site believe in living clean, healthy, sustainable lifestyles that impact the environment as little as possible, and choose to live a dairy-free lifestle. This is very common with millenials, Generation Z, and the health nuts of Generation X. Anyone looking for a high tier and healthier dessert should turn to this site.

The primary task and goals of the people who use this site are to find a sweet treat alternative to ice cream that is healthier for their gut and the environment. They also want an aesthetic experience so that they can post about their clean lifestyle on their social media.
